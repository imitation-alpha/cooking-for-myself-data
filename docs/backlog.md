## Realistic Image Policy for Public Dataset

Status: implemented 2026-05-17

Define the public dataset image policy before open-source release.

- Primary meal images should be realistic home-cooked food photos.
- Primary ingredient images should be realistic grocery or lightly prepped ingredient photos.
- Generated images are acceptable only when they are photo-real, unbranded, disclosed as generated, and reviewed for meal or ingredient accuracy.
- Reject primary assets with icons, illustrations, restaurant branding, packaged-product logos, readable labels, watermarks, visible people, hands, or ingredients that are not represented by the record.
- Add the final policy to `README.md`, `docs/data-contract.md`, `docs/contributing.md`, and schema/validation fields if needed.
